About me

Hello! I am Gabriel Oliveira Alves, a Masters student at the University of São Paulo (USP). I am under the supervision of Prof. Gabriel T. Landi and we are currently working on collisional quantum thermometry, with special focus on Bayesian techniques. My main research interests are quantum information, quantum thermodynamics and quantum computing.

I obtained my Bachelor in Molecular Sciences at the same university. During my undergraduate project, also under the supervision of Prof. Landi, I studied quantum phase transitions in the quantum Rabi model, weak measurements and stochastic master equations.

I have spent five months at the university of Uppsala as an Erasmus exchange student. There, I have worked on holonomic quantum computing under the supervision of Prof. Erik Sjöqvist .
